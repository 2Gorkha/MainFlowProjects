Maggi Recipe

Ingredients: 1 pack of Maggi noodles, 1 cup water, 1 teaspoon oil, 1 Maggi tastemaker, ½ teaspoon red chili powder (optional), ¼ teaspoon black pepper (optional), ½ teaspoon soy sauce (optional), 1 small onion (chopped), 1 small tomato (chopped), ½ cup mixed vegetables (carrot, capsicum, peas - optional), 1 teaspoon butter (optional), 1 tablespoon chopped coriander leaves (for garnish).

Steps: Heat oil in a pan, sauté onions, tomatoes, and vegetables. Add water and bring it to a boil. Break Maggi into pieces and add along with the tastemaker. Mix well and cook for 2-3 minutes. Add optional spices like chili powder, black pepper, and soy sauce for extra flavor. Stir until water is absorbed. Garnish with coriander leaves and serve hot.
