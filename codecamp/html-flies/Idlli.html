Idlli Recipe

Ingredients: 2 cups idli rice, 1 cup urad dal (black gram), Â½ teaspoon fenugreek seeds, salt to taste, water as needed, oil or ghee for greasing.

Steps: Soak idli rice and urad dal separately for 4-6 hours. Grind urad dal into a smooth batter and rice into a slightly coarse batter. Mix both, add salt, and ferment overnight. Grease idli molds, pour batter, and steam for 10-12 minutes. Let cool slightly before serving with chutney and sambar.
